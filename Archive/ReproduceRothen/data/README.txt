rawdata.txt includes the rawdata associated with:

Rothen, N., Jünemann, K., Mealor, A. D., Burckhardt, V., & Ward, J. (2016). The sensitivity and specificity of a diagnostic test of sequence-space synesthesia. Behavior Research Methods, 48(4), 1476–1481. https://doi.org/10.3758/s13428-015-0656-2

columns are named as follows: "Group" "ID" "Inducer" "X" "Y" "Cond"

Group: 		con = controls, syn = synaesthetes
ID:			subject ID
Inducer:	specifies the synaesthetic inducer
X:			x-coordinate of response
Y:			y-coordinate of response
Cond:		specifies the inducer category (number, day, month)
